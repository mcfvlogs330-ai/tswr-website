About Our College

Our college library is a vast hub of knowledge, housing an extensive collection of books dedicated to the three services of Army, Navy, and Air Force. We provide comprehensive materials for Agniveer, SSB (Services Selection Board), SSC (Staff Selection Commission), RRB (Railway Recruitment Board), and all types of Bank exams. Additionally, we stock a wide range of resources to ensure top-tier academic performance for all our students.


Our Infrastructure provides a unique experience: a cool, serene environment by nature, balanced by the "hot" atmosphere of rigorous physical training, ragada, and disciplinary sessions by ADI's and seniors. We believe these challenges forge the strength required for future officers.


Everybody is welcomed to join our journey of discipline, unwavering physical fitness, and leadership training that shapes determined cadets into future visionary officers.
