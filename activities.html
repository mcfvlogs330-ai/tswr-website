NCC & NSS Programs

National Cadet Corps (NCC)

Basic military training in small arms and parades. We develop character, discipline, and the spirit of adventure among young citizens.

National Service Scheme (NSS)

personality development through community service. Our cadets actively participate in social welfare activities and awareness campaigns.

Why Join These Activities?

  • Develop a sense of unity and discipline
  • Enhance leadership and teamwork skills
  • Engage in community service and social outreach
  • Build physical and mental stamina
  • Earn prestigious certificates recognized globally
